About Me

Hello, I am a passionate Ph.D. student in computer science, specializing in computational creativity applied to the dynamic realm of video games. My academic journey has led me to explore the intersection of technology and creativity, aiming to enhance gaming experiences through advanced computational approaches. For my Ph.D. research, my goal is to investigate the feasibility of generating downloadable content (DLC) for metroidvania games based on existing titles. Such a tool could prove valuable for developers and companies seeking to create additional content for users.
